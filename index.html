Welcome to FlyCaucasus!

FlyCaucasus is a virtual airline, intended for flight simulation. It was founded in 2023 by a small group of aviation enthusiasts and has been growing ever since! We aim to provide a valuable experience to all flight simmers. You might ask how?

  1. Friendly, welcoming and supportive environment
  2. A network of scheduled flights across the globe
  3. Variety of fleet
  4. Regular events on online networks such as IVAO
  5. Constant changes, updates and improvements through the feedback of our members

Frequently Asked Questions

What is FlyCaucasus?

FlyCaucasus is a completely fictional, virtual airline for flight simulators.

Which simulators do you support?

We support FSX, Prepar3D, X-Plane 11/12 and MSFS.

Is it required to fly online?

No. It is your choice if you want to fly online or offline, however we often have group flights and events organized on online networks.
